Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Welcome to Vince Batecan State University (VBSU). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the VBSU Enrollment System.

1. Information We Collect

To operate the enrollment system and support student services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, student ID, email address, and contact information.
  • Academic Data: Course information, enrollment records, schedules, grades, and academic history.
  • Account Data: Login credentials, account status, and authentication-related activity.
  • Request Data: Details of forms, requests, reservations, and transactions submitted through the system.
  • Usage Data: System access logs, timestamps, and basic activity records needed for security and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To process enrollments, schedules, and other student records.
  • To manage account access and maintain system security.
  • To process requests, reservations, and related student services.
  • To communicate updates, notices, and transaction confirmations.
  • To maintain institutional records and support administrative reporting.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

VBSU does not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Authorized Personnel: With faculty, staff, or administrators who need access to perform official duties.
  • Service Providers: With third-party providers that support hosting, database, security, or technical operations.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or storage method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to institutional record-keeping requirements and applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal and academic information for as long as necessary to operate the enrollment system, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain institutional records.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy within the application.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the VBSU administration or the system support team.
